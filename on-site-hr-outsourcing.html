Dedicated HR Support at Your Location [Image of office meeting]

SD Staffing Solutions offers human resources support solutions that allow you to concentrate on your core business while we handle the rest with full end-to-end HR solutions.

Our "HR Implant" model involves deploying our dedicated HR professionals to work directly from your office. They seamlessly integrate with your team, handling daily operations such as employee engagement, grievance handling, onboarding, and compliance, all while being backed by our extensive corporate resources.

We enable organizations to reduce costs while still retaining the ability to compete effectively in the marketplace. By outsourcing your on-site HR functions, you convert fixed overheads into manageable variable costs and ensure professional management of your workforce.

Why Choose On-Site HR?

Focus on Core Business

Free up your management team's time from administrative HR tasks so they can focus on strategic growth and core operations.

Cost Reduction

Eliminate the costs of recruiting, training, and retaining an internal HR team. Reduce overheads while maintaining high service levels.

Immediate Resolution

Having an HR professional on-site ensures that employee queries, grievances, and operational issues are resolved in real-time.

Compliance & Expertise

Benefit from our expertise in statutory compliance and labor laws, reducing the risk of legal penalties and non-compliance issues.

How It Works

We assign a qualified HR Executive or Manager to your facility. They report to your management for daily tasks but are on our payroll. We manage their performance, training, and backup, ensuring you have uninterrupted HR support 365 days a year.
